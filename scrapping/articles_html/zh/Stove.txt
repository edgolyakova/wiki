<h1 class="firstHeading mw-first-heading" id="firstHeading"><span class="mw-page-title-main">灶</span></h1>
<div class="vector-body" id="bodyContent">
<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">维基百科，自由的百科全书</div>
<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div id="contentSub2"></div>
<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">跳到导航</a>
<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#searchInput">跳到搜索</a>
<div class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="zh"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note"><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:%E6%B6%88%E6%AD%A7%E4%B9%89" title="Wikipedia:消歧义"><img alt="Disambig gray.svg" data-file-height="168" data-file-width="220" decoding="async" height="19" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5f/Disambig_gray.svg/25px-Disambig_gray.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5f/Disambig_gray.svg/38px-Disambig_gray.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5f/Disambig_gray.svg/50px-Disambig_gray.svg.png 2x" width="25"/></a>  此條目介紹的是一種用來烹飪的固定設施。关于廣義上的煮食用加熱工具，请见「<b><a href="/wiki/%E7%85%AE%E9%A3%9F%E7%88%90" title="煮食爐">煮食爐</a></b>」。</div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="2332" data-file-width="1528" decoding="async" height="336" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/85/%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG/220px-%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/85/%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG/330px-%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/85/%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG/440px-%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84%E7%81%B6%E5%A4%B4.JPG" title="放大"></a></div><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E5%91%A8%E5%BA%84" title="周庄">周庄</a>的中式灶头</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Edo_Kamado.JPG"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="3000" data-file-width="4000" decoding="async" height="165" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/60/Edo_Kamado.JPG/220px-Edo_Kamado.JPG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/60/Edo_Kamado.JPG/330px-Edo_Kamado.JPG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/60/Edo_Kamado.JPG/440px-Edo_Kamado.JPG 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Edo_Kamado.JPG" title="放大"></a></div>日式廚房的灶</div></div></div>
<p><b>灶</b>、<b>爐灶</b>、<b>廚灶</b>或<b>灶頭</b>是一種固定的<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E7%83%B9%E9%A3%AA" title="烹飪">烹飪</a>的設施，透過<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E7%86%B1%E5%82%B3%E5%B0%8E" title="熱傳導">加熱</a><a href="/wiki/%E7%82%8A%E5%85%B7" title="炊具">炊具</a>來達到將食物變熟的目的<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>。中文語境中有時也以灶來指<a href="/wiki/%E7%AA%91" title="窑">窯</a>，例如<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E4%BD%9B%E5%B1%B1" title="佛山">佛山</a>的<a href="/wiki/%E5%8D%97%E9%A3%8E%E5%8F%A4%E7%81%B6" title="南风古灶">南風古灶</a>。
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1944" data-file-width="2592" decoding="async" height="165" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg/220px-Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg/330px-Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg/440px-Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a> <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Indonesian_brick_stove.jpg" title="放大"></a></div>印度尼西亞的磚製灶，這種灶現在還在當地鄉村使用</div></div></div>
<p>早期的灶多是<a href="/wiki/%E9%BB%8F%E5%9C%9F" title="黏土">黏土</a>制灶的，用柴火來加熱。中國在春秋戰國已經有<a href="/wiki/%E7%81%B6%E5%90%9B" title="灶君">灶君</a>出現，<a href="/wiki/%E5%AD%94%E5%AD%90" title="孔子">孔子</a>即有“与其媚于奥，宁媚于灶”之說。<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E5%8F%A4%E5%B8%8C%E8%87%98" title="古希臘">古希臘</a>時也有用來烤麵包的火灶。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bellis_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Bellis-2">[2]</a></sup> 中國和<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E4%B8%96%E7%B4%80" title="中世紀">中世紀</a>的歐洲很早之前就使用磚砌、石製的爐灶來做飯。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Larousse_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Larousse-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>正字為<b>竈</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>。
</p>
