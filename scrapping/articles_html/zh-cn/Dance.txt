<p><b>舞蹈</b>是<a href="/wiki/%E8%A1%A8%E6%BC%94%E8%89%BA%E6%9C%AF" title="表演艺术">表演艺术</a>的一种，由一连串的动作组成，可能是即兴的，也有可能是刻意编排。而这些动作有<a href="/wiki/%E7%BE%8E%E5%AD%A6" title="美学">美学</a>上的价值，也常常有<a href="/wiki/%E8%B1%A1%E5%BE%81%E4%B8%BB%E4%B9%89" title="象征主义">象征性</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[nb 1]</a></sup>。舞蹈可以依其<a href="/wiki/%E5%8B%95%E4%BD%9C%E7%B7%A8%E6%8E%92" title="动作编排">动作编排</a>、动作剧目（repertoire of movements）、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="History of dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="舞蹈的历史"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E7%9A%84%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="舞蹈的历史（页面不存在）">历史时期</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_dance" title="en:History of dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">History of dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>或是其起源来分类<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[4]</a></sup>。
</p><p>在舞蹈上，需要区分<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Concert dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="表演性舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%A1%A8%E6%BC%94%E6%80%A7%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="表演性舞蹈（页面不存在）">表演性舞蹈</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Concert_dance" title="en:Concert dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Concert dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>和<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Participation dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="参与性舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%8F%83%E8%88%87%E6%80%A7%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="参与性舞蹈（页面不存在）">参与性舞蹈</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Participation_dance" title="en:Participation dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Participation dance</span></a></span>）</span></span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[5]</a></sup>，不过这两种分类有时也有重叠之处。两种舞蹈都有其特别的功能，可能是<a href="/wiki/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BA%A4%E8%88%9E" title="社交舞">社交</a>、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="ceremonial dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="仪式舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%84%80%E5%BC%8F%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="仪式舞蹈（页面不存在）">仪式舞蹈</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ceremonial_dance" title="en:ceremonial dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">ceremonial dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="competitive dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="竞技舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E7%AB%B6%E6%8A%80%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="竞技舞蹈（页面不存在）">竞技</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/competitive_dance" title="en:competitive dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">competitive dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>、<a href="/wiki/%E8%89%B7%E8%88%9E" title="艷舞">情色</a>、战舞、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="sacred dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="神圣舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E7%A5%9E%E8%81%96%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="神圣舞蹈（页面不存在）">神圣</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/sacred_dance" title="en:sacred dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">sacred dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>/<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="liturgical dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="敬拜舞蹈"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%95%AC%E6%8B%9C%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="敬拜舞蹈（页面不存在）">宗教敬拜</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/liturgical_dance" title="en:liturgical dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">liturgical dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>。有些活动也有一些类似舞蹈的特点，例如<a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%A6%E8%A1%93" title="武术">武术</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E4%BD%93%E6%93%8D" title="体操">体操</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E7%AB%B6%E6%8A%80%E5%95%A6%E5%95%A6%E9%9A%8A" title="竞技啦啦队">竞技啦啦队</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E8%8A%B1%E6%A0%B7%E6%BB%91%E5%86%B0" title="花样滑冰">花样滑冰</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E8%8A%B1%E6%A0%B7%E6%B8%B8%E6%B3%B3" title="花样游泳">花样游泳</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E8%A1%8C%E9%80%B2%E6%A8%82%E9%9A%8A" title="行进乐队">行进乐队</a>等。
</p>

<h2><span id=".E8.A1.A8.E6.BC.94.E6.80.A7.E8.88.9E.E8.B9.88.E5.92.8C.E5.8F.83.E8.88.87.E6.80.A7.E8.88.9E.E8.B9.88"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="表演性舞蹈和参与性舞蹈">表演性舞蹈和参与性舞蹈</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="编辑章节：表演性舞蹈和参与性舞蹈">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>表演性舞蹈（performance dance）也称戏剧性舞蹈（Theatrical dance）或音乐厅舞蹈（concert dance），是刻意呈现的演出，一般是在<a href="/wiki/%E8%88%9E%E5%8F%B0" title="舞台">舞台</a>上由舞者进行的<a href="/wiki/%E8%A1%A8%E6%BC%94" title="表演">表演</a>。一般会有其<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E6%95%98%E4%BA%8B" title="叙事">叙事</a>、可能配合<a href="/wiki/%E9%BB%98%E5%8A%87" title="默剧">默剧</a>、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Dance costumes" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="舞蹈服饰"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E6%9C%8D%E9%A3%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="舞蹈服饰（页面不存在）">服饰</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dance_costumes" title="en:Dance costumes"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Dance costumes</span></a></span>）</span></span>及<a href="/wiki/%E4%BD%88%E6%99%AF" title="布景">布景</a>，也有可能<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Interpretive dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="形意舞"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%BD%A2%E6%84%8F%E8%88%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="形意舞（页面不存在）">配合音乐伴奏的诠释</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interpretive_dance" title="en:Interpretive dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Interpretive dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>，这些多半是编排过的。这种舞的例子有西方的<a href="/wiki/%E8%8A%AD%E8%95%BE%E8%88%9E" title="芭蕾舞">芭蕾舞</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E7%8F%BE%E4%BB%A3%E8%88%9E" title="现代舞">现代舞</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6%E5%8F%A4%E5%85%B8%E5%BC%8F%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88" title="印度古典式舞蹈">印度古典式舞蹈</a>以及<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="歌剧" data-lang-code="ja" data-lang-name="日语" data-orig-title="日本歌舞剧"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%AD%8C%E8%88%9E%E5%8A%87&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="日本歌舞剧（页面不存在）">日本歌舞剧</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">日语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%AD%8C%E5%8A%87" title="ja:歌剧"><span dir="auto" lang="ja">歌剧</span></a></span>）</span></span>。表演性舞蹈也有可能配合<a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%8C%E5%89%A7" title="歌剧">歌剧</a>或是其他的<a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82%E5%8A%87" title="音乐剧">音乐剧</a>一起出现。
</p><p>参与性舞蹈（Participatory dance）可能是<a href="/wiki/%E6%B0%91%E6%97%8F%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88" title="民族舞蹈">民族舞蹈</a>、<a href="/wiki/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BA%A4%E8%88%9E" title="社交舞">社交舞</a>或<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="group dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="群舞"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E7%BE%A4%E8%88%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="群舞（页面不存在）">群舞</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/group_dance" title="en:group dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">group dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>（例如<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Line dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="排舞"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E6%8E%92%E8%88%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="排舞（页面不存在）">排舞</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Line_dance" title="en:Line dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Line dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>、<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="Circle dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="圆圈舞"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E5%9C%93%E5%9C%88%E8%88%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="圆圈舞（页面不存在）">圆圈舞</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Circle_dance" title="en:Circle dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">Circle dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>、锁链型舞蹈、<a href="/wiki/%E6%96%B9%E5%A1%8A%E8%88%9E" title="方块舞">方块舞</a>或舞伴舞，是大家以共同的目的所进行的舞蹈，目的可能是因为社交、<a href="/wiki/%E9%AB%94%E8%83%BD%E9%8D%9B%E7%85%89" title="体能锻炼">体能锻炼</a>、娱乐等目的，不是为了让其他人观看而进行的。这种舞很少有叙事。其至单人舞也有可能只是为了使舞者自身满足而跳。参与性舞蹈中，一般参与者会有相同的动作和步伐，不过在<a href="/wiki/%E9%9B%BB%E5%AD%90%E8%88%9E%E6%9B%B2" title="电子舞曲">电子舞曲</a>的<a href="/wiki/%E9%8A%B3%E8%88%9E" title="锐舞">锐舞</a>中，舞者可能会是<span class="ilh-all" data-foreign-title="free dance" data-lang-code="en" data-lang-name="英语" data-orig-title="free dance"><span class="ilh-page"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Free_dance&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Free dance（页面不存在）">free dance</a></span><span class="noprint ilh-comment">（<span class="ilh-lang">英语</span><span class="ilh-colon">：</span><span class="ilh-link"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/free_dance" title="en:free dance"><span dir="auto" lang="en">free dance</span></a></span>）</span></span>，不追求和周围的协调。有些文化中可能会在参与性舞蹈中，将一些严格的限制放低，例如男性、女性及小孩可以参与舞蹈。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="历史">历史</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="编辑章节：历史">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">主条目：<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="舞蹈历史（页面不存在）">舞蹈历史</a></div>
<h3><span id=".E8.A5.BF.E6.96.B9"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="西方">西方</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="编辑章节：西方">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>在人类文明起源前，舞蹈在仪式、礼仪、庆典和娱乐方面都十分重要。<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E8%80%83%E5%8F%A4" title="考古">考古</a>提供舞蹈的痕迹，例如9000年前在<a href="/wiki/%E5%8D%B0%E5%BA%A6" title="印度">印度</a>的<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%B3%93%E5%A7%86%E8%B2%9D%E7%89%B9%E5%8D%A1%E7%9A%84%E5%B2%A9%E7%9F%B3%E5%A3%81%E7%95%AB&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="宾姆贝特卡的岩石壁画（页面不存在）">宾姆贝特卡的岩石壁画</a>和公元前3300年<a href="/wiki/%E5%9F%83%E5%8F%8A" title="埃及">埃及</a>墓壁画描绘的绘画舞蹈人物。
</p>
<h3><span id=".E6.9D.B1.E6.96.B9"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="东方">东方</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="编辑章节：东方">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E5%8D%8E%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%91%E5%85%B1%E5%92%8C%E5%9B%BD" title="中华人民共和国">中国</a>在五千年以前就已经出现了舞蹈，<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cqw_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-cqw-7">[6]</a></sup>产生于<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E5%A5%B4%E9%9A%B8%E7%A4%BE%E6%9C%83" title="奴隶社会">奴隶社会</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-co_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-co-8">[7]</a></sup>，发展到秦汉之际已形成一定特色：长袖细腰、飘逸轻盈、技艺并重、刚柔相济。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-co_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-co-8">[7]</a></sup>魏晋南北朝以来中外乐舞的大交流以及唐代帝王的重视，<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E6%A5%8A%E8%B2%B4%E5%A6%83" title="杨贵妃">杨贵妃</a>等舞蹈家的出现，使中国表演性舞蹈空前繁荣。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-co_8-2"><a href="#cite_note-co-8">[7]</a></sup>
</p><p>宋元明清以后，中国的表演性舞蹈逐渐融入戏曲，成为塑造角色、表达情感、展示剧情的<a href="/wiki/%E6%88%8F%E6%9B%B2" title="戏曲">戏曲</a>化舞蹈。
</p><p>中华人民共和国建立后，作为独立的剧场艺术的舞蹈日益完善和成熟起来。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cqw_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-cqw-7">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span id=".E7.89.B9.E8.89.B2"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="特色">特色</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="编辑章节：特色">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>每一个舞蹈有它的主题，而这主题也要进行发展和变化。这和<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82" title="音乐">音乐</a>的情形是很相似的。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cr_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-cr-9">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id=".E6.8B.89.E4.B8.81.E8.88.9E"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="拉丁舞">拉丁舞</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="编辑章节：拉丁舞">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">主条目：<a href="/wiki/%E6%8B%89%E4%B8%81%E8%88%9E" title="拉丁舞">拉丁舞</a></div>
<h3><span id=".E4.B8.AD.E5.9C.8B.E8.88.9E.E8.B9.88"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="中国舞蹈">中国舞蹈</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="编辑章节：中国舞蹈">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">主条目：<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88" title="中国舞蹈">中国舞蹈</a></div>
<p>中国舞蹈有一个明显的特点，那就是「构图」的对称性，左边的人做了一种姿势，右边的人也做一种相应的姿势。舞蹈的三要素是指表情（不单是脸部的表情，是全身的表情）、节奏和构图。中国舞蹈的表情和节奏各有特点，但特征最显着的是构图。这种构图在舞蹈中叫做「对称的平衡」。<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cr_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-cr-9">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span id=".E5.88.86.E9.A1.9E"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="分类">分类</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="编辑章节：分类">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>根据舞蹈的功能、用途，可分「实用舞蹈」（自娱）与「表演舞蹈」（娱人）两大类；每大类下，再根据「时间」分为传统与流行，共计即四类；
然后可再往下细分但无论那一个分类方法，其界线均已模糊，分类表示如下。
</p>

<h2><span id=".E8.A8.BB.E8.A7.A3"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="注解">注解</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=%E8%88%9E%E8%B9%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="编辑章节：注解">编辑</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">
<ol class="references">
<li id="cite_note-4"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-4">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text">有许多有关舞蹈的定义。定义包括以下这些：<br/><br/>
「舞蹈是人类因为美学诉求所创作并且表演的动作」（Dance is human movement created and expressed for an aesthetic purpose）<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Fraleigh1987_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Fraleigh1987-1">[1]</a></sup><br/><br/>
「舞蹈是人体在空间中运动时，以特定的形式及风格所进行的短暂呈现。舞蹈一般会是刻意选择，而有控制的节奏运动。所产生的结果被表演者以及特定群体中的观众认为是舞蹈。」（Dance is a transient mode of expression performed in a given form and style by the human body moving in space. Dance occurs through purposefully selected and controlled rhythmic movements; the resulting phenomenon is recognized as dance both by the performer and the observing members of a given group.）<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><br/><br/>
「舞蹈是由有目的的（包括个人选择以及社会学习）、刻意有节奏旳、以及有文化模式的非语言人体动作组成（以舞者的观点，多半也被和舞者相同文化的观众所接受），而且这些动作和日常的动作中不同。动作（在时间、空间或是心力上）有其内在美学上的价值（在舞者文化上是以适当的方式表达，而且有其潜力），也有其象征性的潜力。」（Dance is human behaviour composed (from the dancer’s perspective, which is usually shared by the audience members of the dancer’s culture) of purposeful (individual choice and social learning play a role), intentionally rhythmical, and culturally patterned sequences of nonverbal body movement mostly other than those performed in ordinary motor activities. The motion (in time, space, and with effort) has an inherent and aesthetic value (the notion of appropriateness and competency as viewed by the dancer’s culture) and symbolic potential.）<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></span>
</li>
</ol></div>
